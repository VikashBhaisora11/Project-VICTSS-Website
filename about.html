About Us

VICTSS Pvt Ltd is an Award winning Odoo ERP development and Consulting  company that has been successfully present on the software development market for over 10+ years and since that time has grown to become a well-regarded player in this industry and has proven to be a reliable, efficient and trustworthy service provider to the businesses from all over the World.

The company was established recognizing the potential of the increasing market of the companies, that are or would be looking to make their business processes more efficient by automating them through the use of advanced software solutions and/or to act in response to the current market forces that dictate the necessity of online presence in order to stay abreast with competition.

Since the time it was founded in the year 2012, the company has been involved in releasing numerous projects for diverse business spheres ranging from basic websites to highly sophisticated business automation systems. As the Gold Partner of Odoo,  we offer end-to-end Odoo ERP Implementation to achieve the correct solution for the better usefulness and profitability
